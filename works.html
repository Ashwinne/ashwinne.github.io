C. Ashwinne is currently writing The Promise of Thunder, an adult fantasy novel that explores war, the people who fight, and the relationships that form during the bloodshed. Set in the Franco-German inspired country of Rhiennelent, The Promise of Thunder is the story of nineteen-year-old Anya Durand, a mute sorceress who runs away to join the military to find answers after her home is destroyed.
