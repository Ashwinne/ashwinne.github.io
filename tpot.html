The Promise of Thunder

Fourteen years ago, Anya Durand lost her father and her voice. Now at nineteen, her village and her mentor have been torn away from her in a cruel act of war. She runs away to join the military's sorcerer program in order to provide for her family and to try to find the cure for the curse that prevents her from speaking. Instead, she finds herself drawn into the same web of corruption and blackmail that took her father's life, all while fighting feelings for her sign language interpreter.
